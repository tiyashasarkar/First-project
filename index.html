One quick setup step

Blossom needs a free Firebase account to sign you in and keep your memories synced. Open js/firebase-config.js and paste in your project's values — full steps are in README.md under "Setting up your free account".

Welcome to Blossom

A cozy, private scrapbook for your photos and memories — soft, personal, and made for your phone.

📸

Turn photos into pages

Pick photos from your library, arrange them freely, and decorate with tape, stickers and handwritten notes.

📔

Keep it all in journals

Group your pages into journals — a travel diary, a daily journal, or just random happy moments.

🔒

Just for you

Your memories are private to your account, securely synced so they're always there — on this phone or any other device you sign into.
